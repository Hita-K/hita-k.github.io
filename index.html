About

I am currently studying Information Systems, Computer Science, and Machine Learning at Carnegie Mellon Universtiy. My research interests include end user robotics programming, computer vision-based systems for improved accessibility, and deep learning systems to better software engineering testing. These amalgamate to an overarching goal—utilizing techniques such as computer vision and deep learning to build systems for end users or atypical users.

With my previous research experience in deep learning, computer vision, and human-robot interaction, I want to now explore the field of bioinformatics. I have always been fascinated by healthcare, specifically personalized healthcare, wearable health-monitoring devices, robotics technologies for biomedical applications, and deep learning systems for medical image classification.
